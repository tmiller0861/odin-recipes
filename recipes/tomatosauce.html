Tomato Sauce

Description

This is a simple tomato sauce made on the stove top appropriate for pastas, pizza, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 4 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Oliver Oil
  • Oregano
  • Red Crushed Pepper
  • Salt/Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt Oil and Butter in saute pan over medium heat
  2. Add tomatoes, oregano, and red crushed pepper, bring to boil
  3. Simmer sauce until thickened and taste turns from fresh tomatoes to sauce
  4. Salt and Pepper to taste
