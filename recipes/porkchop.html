ACV Pork Chops

Description

Oven Braised Pork Chops in Apple Cider Vinegar till fork tender

Ingredients

  • Thick Pork Chops
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Chicken Stock
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Salt/Pepper

Steps

  1. Coat Pork Chops with flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs
  2. Brown Pork over medium heat in oil
  3. Saute sliced onion and garlic in oil and worcestershire sauce
  4. Place Chops in Dutch oven and cover with sauteed onions and garlic, add enough ACV and chicken stock to cover
  5. Place in Oven at 300-325 for 1:30-2 hours
